Where the numbers come from

A transparent pipeline, filing to figure.

01 · SOURCE

SEC EDGAR XBRL

10-K / 10-Q filings ingested straight from each company's own XBRL, joined to market prices.

02 · NORMALIZE

Comparable metrics

Quarters reconciled to annuals, then margins, leverage, FCF, and valuation multiples computed uniformly.

03 · VALUE

Intrinsic value

Four methods priced at each fiscal close, with a margin of safety versus the market.

04 · TEST

Backtest

Archetype screens replayed across history as of each date only — never with hindsight.
